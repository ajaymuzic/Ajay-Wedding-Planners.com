BISHOP HEBER COLLEGE

College Of Arts And Science

BISHOP HEBER COLLEGE is a religious minority educational insitutional in Tiruchirappalli,TamilNadu,India.It was founded in 1966,althrough the school has historical roots extending through various earlier local religious schools to about the mid-nineteenth century.Bishop Heber College offers several undergraduate and graduate degrees including the doctorate level

Traning And Placement

Training and Placement Division of Bishop Heber College has an unmatched record in campus placement . The Division endeavours to provide inputs that will help the students to enhance their employability skills and carve a niche for themselves in the global arena.Intensive Training Programmes and workshops are designed and conducted by Training and Placement Division to professionalize students in relation to their fields. The Division takes an extra effort to closely monitor the perfomance of the students who have applied for jobs and it also helps them in devising materials and getting ready for interviews and job talks. Crash courses on soft skills are also conducted to fine tune the employability skills of the students. 1417 final year students have benefited by using the facilities offered by Training and Placement Division . 451 students have been placed in prestigious companies such as TCS, CTS,Infosys,Reliance etc in the academic year 2019-2020. Companies such as s TCS, CTS,Infosys,Reliance,Deloitte etc frequently visit heber campus to recruite students. Students are also encouraged to attend off campus interviews.

Arts college

Arts Colleges were introduced in 1997 as part of the now defunct Specialist Programme in the United Kingdom. The system enabled secondary schools to specialise in certain fields, in this case, the performing, visual and/or media arts. Schools that successfully applied to the Specialist Schools Trust and became Arts Colleges received extra funding from this joint private sector and government scheme. Arts Colleges also acted as a local point of reference for other schools and businesses in the area, with an emphasis on promoting art within the community.
